Game Case Video

Game Requirements

Sentences in the present continuous or future tense must be used, the "thing" that is being done reflects the place of the member, the weather, etc., the correct verbs and phrases must be used, and the expression can be more lively and interesting.
(必须使用现在进行时态或将来时的句子；表达出正在做的“事情”体现该成员所在的地点、天气等情况；使用正确的动词和短语；表达可以更生动有趣)

Explanation of the "Presenter Contest" (游戏方法)

Work in groups, the group selects one person to be the presenter, and the other group members start to do their own "thing".
（分组合作，小组选出一人作为主持人，其他小组成员开始做自己的“事情”；主持人介绍画面情景，可以采访正在活动的小组成员；其他成员可以回答或者提问主持人，也可以跟其他成员互动。）

The presenter introduces the screen scene and can interview the group members who are in the activity.

Other members can answer or ask questions to the presenter, and they can also interact with other members.

How the "Best Presenter" is selected

Illustrate

After the end of the competition, each group has two votes, and the teacher has one vote, and each group will discuss and vote for scoring, and they are not allowed to vote for their own group.
(比赛结束后，每个小组两票，老师一票，由各个小组商议投票打分，不得给自己小组投票。)
